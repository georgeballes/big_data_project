Project Overview

This dashboard presents the results of a Big Data Analytics project that demonstrates proficiency with Databricks, Apache Spark, and cloud data workflows. The project uses the Online Retail dataset to perform customer segmentation using RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) analysis and machine learning.

Project Components:

  • Data ingestion from a public dataset into a data lake structure
  • Data cleaning and transformation using Apache Spark
  • RFM analysis for customer segmentation
  • Machine learning pipeline with MLflow for clustering
  • Interactive visualizations and business insights

Dataset Information:

The Online Retail dataset contains all transactions occurring between 01/12/2010 and 09/12/2011 for a UK-based online retail company. The company mainly sells unique all-occasion gifts, and many customers are wholesalers.

Business Insights and Recommendations

Customer Segmentation Overview

Our analysis has identified 3 distinct customer segments:

  • Champions (20.1% of customers): Recent purchasers with high frequency and monetary value. These are your best customers who bought recently, buy often and spend the most. Focus on retention and loyalty programs for this segment.
  • Regular Customers (56.4% of customers): Customers with moderate recency, frequency, and monetary values. These customers are regular shoppers but have potential to increase their spending. Target them with personalized offers and upselling.
  • Hibernating Customers (23.5% of customers): Customers who haven't purchased in a long time. These customers are at risk of churning or have already churned. Re-engagement campaigns and win-back offers should be directed at this segment.
Revenue and Customer Value Insights

Key business metrics from our analysis:

  • Total Revenue: $9,747,747.93
  • Average Order Value: $376.36
  • Customer Lifetime Value: $2,229.59
  • Top Country by Revenue: United Kingdom

The Champions segment, while only 20.1% of the customer base, likely contributes a disproportionately high percentage of total revenue. This highlights the importance of retention strategies for high-value customers.

Strategic Recommendations

Based on our customer segmentation analysis, we recommend the following strategies:

  1. For Champions:
    • Implement a VIP loyalty program with exclusive benefits
    • Provide early access to new products
    • Create a referral program to leverage their network
    • Gather feedback to improve products and services
  2. For Regular Customers:
    • Develop personalized product recommendations based on purchase history
    • Implement targeted email campaigns with relevant offers
    • Create bundle offers to increase average order value
    • Encourage more frequent purchases with limited-time promotions
  3. For Hibernating Customers:
    • Launch re-engagement campaigns with special "we miss you" discounts
    • Conduct surveys to understand reasons for inactivity
    • Showcase new products or improvements since their last purchase
    • Consider a win-back program with incentives for returning

Additionally, consider implementing the following general strategies:

  • Develop a customer journey map for each segment to identify touchpoints for improvement
  • Set up automated marketing workflows based on RFM segments
  • Regularly update the segmentation model with new transaction data
  • Monitor segment migration to measure the effectiveness of marketing strategies
